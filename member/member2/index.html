Introduction

I conduct research on the interaction between aerosols, clouds, and radiation. 25 papers have been published, of which 8 have been published as first or corresponding authors, including 7 SCI (RSER, GRL, JGR, ERL and other journals), with a cumulative impact factor of 31.55+14. The main work achievements are as follows:

  1. Reveal the chemical mechanism behind the slow decrease in winter PM2.5 in eastern China under emission reduction measures, and find that emission reduction measures lead to a rapid increase in surface solar radiation;
  2. Improve the simulation capability of smoke aerosol models and evaluate their impact on air quality and radiation; 3. Observation and exploration of the generation and dissipation characteristics of shallow convective clouds and cloud radiation effects on the plateau
